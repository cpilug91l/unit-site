About UNIT

UNIT is conceived as a digital utility infrastructure within Web3 ecosystems, connecting applications, services and users in a flexible and scalable way. The token does not represent legal tender, does not grant equity in any company and is not issued or backed by a government, central bank or financial institution.

The aim of UNIT is to explore modern models of digitalisation, payments, interoperability and on-chain governance in a transparent and verifiable framework that can evolve together with its community and integrations.

Utility in the ecosystem
  • Digital payments within the UNIT ecosystem
    Fast value transfer with reduced costs in applications that integrate UNIT as a means of access, fees or internal settlement.
  • Interoperability (conceptual, in development)
    UNIT is designed to be usable in multi-protocol architectures and Web3 applications that need a common digital utility layer across services.
  • Staking & governance
    Holders may participate, in future implementations, in staking mechanisms and on-chain governance processes within the UNIT ecosystem and compatible applications.
  • Controlled burn mechanics
    Possibility to reduce circulating supply over time, depending on governance decisions and the economic design chosen for the ecosystem.
  • Digitalisation & tokenisation
    UNIT can be integrated into prototypes and projects that explore digitalisation of processes, digital identities or tokenisation of assets within experimental or sandbox environments.
