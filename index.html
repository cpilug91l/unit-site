How to buy

Access UNIT safely on BNB Smart Chain.

The steps below describe a generic, non-custodial way to access UNIT. Always verify the correct contract address and make sure you understand self-custody before sending any funds.

1

Set up a BSC wallet

Install a trusted non-custodial wallet (for example MetaMask) and add the BNB Smart Chain network. Securely back up your Seed / Secret Recovery Phrase offline. Never share it with anyone.

2

Fund with BNB

Acquire a small amount of BNB on a reputable exchange and withdraw it to your BSC wallet address. You will need BNB to pay network transaction fees and to swap into UNIT on a DEX.

3

Swap for UNIT

Use a DEX that supports BNB Smart Chain. Import the UNIT contract 0x4f5710c96C446b133C59Af006A366d07414B52a9, double-check every character, then swap a chosen amount of BNB or another supported token into UNIT.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

A short overview of how UNIT is positioned. For jurisdiction-specific details, always check with local professionals and regulators.

Is UNIT an investment?

No. UNIT is a digital utility and access token. While it can be traded on secondary markets, there is no promise of profit, dividends or capital protection. Price is entirely determined by open markets.

Who controls UNIT?

UNIT is deployed as a smart contract on BNB Smart Chain. Once parameters are set and ownership, if applicable, is renounced according to the contract, there is no central party that can arbitrarily change supply or seize user funds. Coordination happens transparently via on-chain activity and public channels.

Can the rules change later?

Smart contract rules are enforced by the blockchain and cannot be changed unless explicitly coded in an upgradeable structure. Off-chain policies (how applications integrate UNIT) may evolve to stay aligned with security best practices and regulatory guidance.

Do I need KYC to hold UNIT?

Holding UNIT in a self-custodial wallet typically does not require KYC. However, exchanges, fiat on-ramps, off-ramps and certain jurisdictions may impose KYC / AML requirements. Always follow the rules of the platforms and countries you use.
